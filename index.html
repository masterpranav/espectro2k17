DANCE:GHOOMAR RELOADED

‘Draw in a breath, kick off your shoes, and dance’-Oprah Winfrey. Put your hand on your heart and listen to your soul. Intensify the floor, express your thoughts and enrapture the audience’s heart with your moves.Classical and folk, Hip Hop and Hollywood, Contemporary and Duet at Espectro,we have them all !! Ready, Set, Dance people!!!!

RANGOLI KA MANDANA

Rangolis are something each of us can easily relate to as they signify two important things - Colors, which we Indians love and Festivities, which we Indians enjoy with our families. Be it any occasion, a family function or a festival, a Rangoli can lift the mood of everyone and can fetch you some quick compliments. It’s a great way to add colour by adding some glamour to Espectro 2k17

PRONITE

And here's the grandest of them all! Our showstopper concert which will leave you dumbfounded. Experience your idols right before your eyes and get mesmerized by their tunes. Arijit Singh, Badshaah, J-Star are just few of the names that have graced this stage in the past few years. Who's it going to be this year? Stay tuned to know more!

BARFI

Emotions don't need words; a blink of the eye, a tiny nod of the head, a curve on the lips, can say it all.'Barfi' revolves around the art of expressing and comphrehending what has never been said.

Unveil the mask – Face painting

The most important kind of freedom is to be what you really are. You trade in your reality for a role. You give up your ability to feel, and in exchange, put on a mask.

SINGING: SURILO KESARIYO

It is said that a soulful voice can bring even the toughest of us to tears. Singing has been the cornerstone of music for time immemorial, from Tansen in Akbar's court to the likes of Asha Bhonsle and Lata Mangeshkar. So all the singers out there, please come and spread the joy this April in Espectro 2k17 by showing off your swar, raga and taal! To the shy ones out there, we promise you, this will be one of the most fun experiences of your lives!

FASHION SHOW: PEHNAVA

Pehnava probably one of the events in Espectro where you can experience the peak of glamour and grace, conviction and jitter and impressive style . We bring to you Lifestyle, a fashion show competition filled with glamour and panache.. So ready yourself for all this glamour .

BLOGGING: UNSCRIPTED

We’ve all spent ages trying to put together a few ideas and get that story together. Have you always wished to be the next Roald Dahl, the next JK Rowling or the next GRR Martin? Put together a plot, give the lead a love story and defeat the evil overlord. Drop by Espectro and pen down what runs through that imaginative little head of yours. So, unleash your ideas onto this world with a single seam of paper and a pen to wield!

Photography event: Lights,Camera,Action

Make this world your playground, play around with the lens and come up with exquisite photographs from specific fields, photographs that are full of life, photographs that can move our souls!

STREET PLAY : MIMANSA

A group of people sending a message to the society about a social cause with their wellcrafted dialogues and punch-lines. Seems familiar? We bring to you the street play event of Rendezvous. Huge groups coming together to get their message across. Music, drums, costumes and cheers. Seems like your kind of thing? Don't miss out on fun, go watch the event as an audience to enjoy the mesmerizing performances brought to you by Espectro 2k17!

About

Espectro

Espectro is an annual cultural fest of MBM Engineering College, Jodhpur, organized by student union. This fest is being celebrated in college from past 5 years. Over 5000 youth take part in this fest and enjoy this.

Again this year, Student union of college is organizing "ESPECTRO - A DIVE INTO ECSTASY !" with an event theme of "Padharo Mhare desh"
